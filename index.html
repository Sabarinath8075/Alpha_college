About:

Alpha College is a trusted educational institution offering specialized courses and tuition for students pursuing SSLC (Class 10) and +2 (Class 12) through NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling).

6-Month SSLC/+2 courses:

Alpha College offers a 6-month intensive program for both SSLC and +2 students. This unique fast-track course is designed especially for those who are seeking to finish their secondary or higher secondary education efficiently. It is ideal for students who could not complete regular schooling due to various reasons.

Our courses are recognized and valid for government and competitive exams such as PSC, UPSC. Students can complete their education and appear for these exams without delay.

What We Offer:

  • NIOS X and XII Courses (6-Month Fast Track).

  • Specialized coaching in Humanities, Commerce, and Science.

  • Special tuition for key subjects like Mathematics, Science, and Commerce.

  • Complete academic support with study materials, project guidance, and personal mentoring.

  • We help students not just pass exams, but also build confidence for their academic and career growth.
